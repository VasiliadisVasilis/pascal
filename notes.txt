--
Komvoi me 2 telesteous
--
  0) Oi 2 telesteoi einai apo8hkeumenoi se kataxwrhtes
    Auth einai h eukolh lush, desmevw enan neo kataxwrhth gia to apotelesma kai ekxwrw se auton to
    apotelesma tou operation
  1) Enas ek twn telestewn einai sta8era
    addi, subi ktlp dexontai ws 2o orisma th sta8era MONO efoson auth mporei na apo8hkeutei se 16 bits
    se opoiadhpote allh periptwsh desmevw enan kataxwrhth gia th sta8era kai thn antigrafw se auton
    ( dhladh dhmiourgw enan neo komvo )
  2) Kai oi 2 telesteoi einai sta8eres
    Desmevw 2 kataxwrhtes kai antigrafw se ka8ena apo autous mia sta8era, epeita xrhsimopoiw tous
    kataxwrhtes ws orismata sto operation
  Na dwsw prosoxh sth sta8era 0 ( auth brisketai panta ston kataxwrhth $0 )\

Klhseis Routinwn kai registers
--
  Meta apo mia jal, oloi kataxwrhtes ( ektos twn S ) 8ewrountai akuroi, an 8elw na xrhsimopoihsw kapoion
  kataxwrhth pou exei parei timh prin apo jal 8a prepei autos na einai SAVED kataxwrhths.

  Den mporw omws na kanw oles tis ana8eseis mou se saved kataxwrhtes giati pi8anon na mhn xreiazetai
  na 8umamai tis times tous.

  SE PERIPTWSH POU APALOIFW KOINES YPOEKFRASEIS

  tou kwdika xrhsimopoiountai "polles fores" kai etsi na ana8esw ta pio dhmofilh apotelesmata se
  kataxwrhtes tupou SAVED. ( MONO EFOSON uparxei klhsh sunarthshs, alliws me 'volevei' kai aplh
  ana8esh se typou TEMP kataxwrhth )
